Andy Warhol (1928-1987) was an American Popartist born Andrew Warhola in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.

Andy is most known for his visual art as a painter and printmaker but wore many hats.

Early Years (1949-1961)

Andy's career began as an illustrator for magazines and advertisements. As he gained fame for these ink drawings and the vinyl record was introduced, RCA Records took notice, hiring him to design album covers and promotional materials.
